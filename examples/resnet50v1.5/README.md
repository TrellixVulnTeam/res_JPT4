## Accuracy


| **Model** | **AMP Top1** | **AMP Top5** | **FP32 Top1** | **FP32 Top1** |
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| resnet50 | 78.46 | 94.15 | 78.50 | 94.11 |
| resnext101-32x4d | 80.08 | 94.89 | 80.14 | 95.02 |
